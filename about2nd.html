About Cascade

The Cascade Café, established in August 2024, is nestled within the vibrant campus of Holy Angel University, making it a convenient and central spot for students, faculty, and visitors alike. The café was thoughtfully designed with students in mind, serving as not just a place to grab a bite, but as a hub where people can gather, whether they're looking for a cozy corner to study, a relaxed space to unwind between classes, or simply a welcoming environment to catch up with friends. One of the standout features that sets Cascade Café apart is its flexibility. Understanding the diverse needs of the university community, the café offers a unique level of freedom—students are welcome to bring in outside food or drinks, provided they also make a purchase from the café. This policy fosters an open, inclusive environment where everyone feels comfortable, making it an ideal spot for group projects, casual hangouts, or solo study sessions.

Mission

To provide customers with a satisfying experience, ensuring that every visit leaves them refreshed and content. The café aims to be more than just a place for food; it’s a space that adds color to daily life, with its warm ambiance, aesthetic décor, and inviting atmosphere.

Vision

Tocontinue delivering vibrant, enriching moments for students and visitors alike, making it a staple of campus life for years to come.
